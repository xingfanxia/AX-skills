# Evidence Ladder Template

| Level | Name | Use | Examples |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 0 | Noise / lead only | Generate questions | Social posts, return screenshots, anonymous chatter, price action |
| 1 | Weak signal | Support only with corroboration | Marketing pages, generic decks, unnamed customer claims |
| 2 | Medium signal | Support thesis if triangulated | Customer/supplier pages, roadmaps, grants, trade publications, adjacent earnings calls |
| 3 | Strong signal | Material support | Filings, named contracts, concrete capacity disclosures, design wins with timing |
| 4 | Confirmed economic capture | Highest conviction | Multi-quarter revenue conversion, margin expansion, backlog conversion, customer-confirmed dependency |
